Welcome To ClimbCircle
ClimbCircle is a platform that aggregates access to rock climbing and bouldering gyms across Singapore.
Instead of purchasing multiple gym membership packages, subscribers can choose to pay a lower monthly fee to gain access to a wider range of rock climbing facilities and functionalities of our mobile application.
We offer all functionalities needed to run your gym!
